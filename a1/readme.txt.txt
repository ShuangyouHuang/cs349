This is a simple breakout game.
Your goal is to prevent the ball from touching the bottom line of the window and break all bricks.
Use "A" to move the paddle leftward, use "D" to move the paddle rightward.
Use "Q" or "ESC" to quit the game.
There are bricks with different colors:
blue bricks need four hit to break,
yellow ones need three hits,
brown ones need two hits,
pink ones only need one hit.
When the game is over (either you win or lose), press "R" to restart the game.
